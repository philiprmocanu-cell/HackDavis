# Heart-rate measurement call — TTS scripts (non-diagnostic)

Emergency-first copy for an outbound **phone** line. The service uses the handset mic in a **rough** acoustic test—not a stethoscope or medical device. Outputs are **not** a diagnosis.

## intro_english

If you have severe chest pain, crushing pressure, pain spreading to your arm or jaw, sudden shortness of breath, fainting, or you feel you might be having an emergency: hang up and call your local emergency number now, or go to the nearest emergency department.

This is an automated, experimental line—not a doctor. If you still want to continue, you may try a rough heart-sound check. Press the phone gently against your chest, near the heart, with the microphone area touching the skin if you can. Stay very quiet. We will listen for about thirty seconds starting now.
